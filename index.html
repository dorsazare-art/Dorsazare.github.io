Game Design





















Play Emotional Support Pet Game

Title of the work: Emotional Support Pet
Date of completion: 2023
Medium: Web Program - JavaScript

Intention My game project, created as part of the Creative Computation course in the Computational Arts department, aims to provide players with a unique avenue for emotional exploration and self-expression. I wanted to develop a game that goes beyond mere entertainment and offers players a therapeutic experience to engage with their emotions in a meaningful way.

Process: Using Procreate for visual design and JavaScript for programming, I crafted a game where players are prompted to reflect on their current emotional state. Based on their feelings, the game suggests mini-games tailored to their emotions. For example, if a player is feeling angry, they might be guided to a calming painting game where they can release their emotions by virtually painting a wall. The emphasis was on creating a stress-free and satisfying experience that encourages players to express themselves authentically rather than escape from their feelings. This project represents my commitment to using technology as a tool for emotional healing.














Play "Save the Environment" Game

Title of the work: Save the Environment
Date of completion: 2023
Medium: Web Program - JavaScript

Intention I created "Save the Environment" game as part of the Creative Computation course in the Computational Arts department. This game is designed to immerse players in small, impactful actions to combat climate change. It aims to demonstrate the ease and effectiveness of making a difference by engaging in virtual tasks like cleaning up garbage and planting trees, with the intention of inspiring real-life action.

Process: Illustrated using Procreate and programmed with JavaScript, the game prompts players to choose actions to help the environment. After completing tasks, players are encouraged to replicate their actions in real life, leveraging the game experience to enact positive change. This project underscores my commitment to using technology to promote environmental awareness and activism.




3D Modelling






Different angles of this 3D Design






Title: Living Room
Date of completion: 2023
Medium: Digital Art - Blender

Intention: This is a Personal Project that I worked on since I am passionate about creating 3D environments.
Process: The Blender software was used to create this 3D Living Room. The modelling approach is described briefly here. I began with the main geometry and then individually adjusted the shapes in edit mode. I chose blue and yellow as the main colours for this living room, which is different from the tutorial. I changed the texture of the objects, particularly the curtains, to make them more transparent, and I increased the metallic level of the light fixture. Two area lights were then installed to brighten the space and background. The camera was then adjusted and rendered from various angles.

This is the tutorial I used for my project.

Title: Bedroom
Date of completion: 2023
Medium: Digital Art - Blender

Intention: This is a Personal Project that I worked on in order to create another type of 3D room with a different theme.
Process: This 3D bedroom was created using Blender. I started with the walls and the floor, then added each piece of furniture and item individually and tweaked the shapes in edit mode. I also changed the colours and textures. For this bedroom, I chose green as the base colour and added wood to the furniture and yellow to the objects to make the room look warmer and cozier. Finally, I adjusted the lights and camera and rendered the model.

Title: Mushrooms in a bottle
Date of completion: 2023
Medium: Digital Art - Blender

Intention: This was a personal project that I worked on since I love making imaginative 3D environments.
Process: Blender software was used to build this model. I started with the mushrooms and small objects around it, then added the bottle. I changed the material of the bottle to make it look like glass and eliminated some of the light reflections. I added stars and light circles to the background to make it more interesting. The blurry light circles effect was created by drawing little circles and positioning them very close to the camera. When the camera's focus is on the primary item, the small circles become blurry, resulting in this lens flare effect.

Title: Fox
Date of completion: 2023
Medium: Digital Art - Blender


Intention: This is a Personal Project that I worked on to learn more about creating 3D characters.
Process: This 3D fox character was produced in Blender. I started by making the fox's overall head and body, added the details, such as the tail, white portions, and eyes, etc., and then individually changed the colours and textures of each part. I added the bottom green circle and added a background to the character to help it stand out on a surface. Lastly I added two area lights to either side of the character.
